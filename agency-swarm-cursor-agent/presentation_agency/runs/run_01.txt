 2 Challenges in Throughput Estimation:                                                                                                                                      
    • Throughput fluctuations are common due to the nature of TCP, the underlying protocol of HTTP.

                                                                        Key Formulas and Explanations                                                                        

 1 Throughput Estimation Method:                                                                                                                                             
    • The throughput for a segment ( T_s(i) ) is calculated using the request-response duration.
